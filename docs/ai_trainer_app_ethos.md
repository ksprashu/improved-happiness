## II. Exercise Science Principles

### 1. **Fat Burn & EPOC**

* Prioritize **large muscle groups** â†’ legs, glutes, back, chest.
* Use **compound movements** â†’ multi-joint exercises recruiting multiple muscles.
* High intensity, low rest = maximal post-exercise oxygen demand (EPOC).
* Alternate push/pull and lower/upper movements for total-body recruitment.

### 2. **Progressive Overload**

* Track and increment:

  * Load (weight or resistance)
  * Volume (sets/reps)
  * Density (shorter rest intervals)
  * Complexity (multi-planar moves)
* AI should ensure non-repetitive patterning: avoid hitting the same muscle group intensely within 48 hours.

### 3. **Periodization**

Dynamic daily programming:

* **Micro-sessions (10â€“20 min):** Flow + activation
* **Core sessions (25â€“40 min):** Strength + conditioning
* **Macro-sessions (45â€“60 min):** Full-body + skill + recovery integration
